Disclaimer · educational use only

Fixed constants: PH₂O = 47 mmHg at 37 °C. Normal PAO₂ on room air at sea level ≈ 99–104 mmHg; an A–a gradient below ~15 mmHg is generally normal in young adults and rises with age and supplemental O₂. This is the simplified alveolar gas equation and does not model shunt, V/Q heterogeneity, or diffusion limitation.

For learning the equation only — not a substitute for a real ABG, clinical judgment, or the patient's full context. Verify anything clinically actionable against primary sources.
